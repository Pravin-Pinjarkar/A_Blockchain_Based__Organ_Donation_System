#  A Blockchain-Based Decentralized Organ Donation & Supply Chain Management System

## Project Overview

The Blockchain-Based Decentralized Organ Donation System is a secure platform that makes the organ donation process more transparent, reliable, and efficient. It uses blockchain technology to securely store donor, recipient, and hospital records, ensuring that data cannot be changed or misused.

The system helps reduce fraud, improves trust among hospitals and patients, and makes the organ donation process faster and more secure.

---

##  Objectives

- Make organ donation records transparent and secure.
- Prevent unauthorized access and data tampering.
- Provide secure donor and recipient registration.
- Maintain permanent and reliable medical records.
- Improve coordination between donors, recipients, hospitals, and authorities.

---

##  Key Features

- Secure blockchain-based storage of donation records.
- Donor and recipient registration with authentication.
- Hospital and authority verification.
- Smart contract-based organ matching and allocation.
- Real-time tracking of the organ donation process.
- Tamper-proof medical records.
- Secure and transparent data sharing.
- Complete audit trail of every transaction.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Blockchain:** Ethereum
- **Smart Contracts:** Solidity
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Wallet:** MetaMask
- **Blockchain Library:** Ethers.js

---

## Benefits

- Increases trust and transparency.
- Reduces fraud and fake records.
- Provides secure data storage.
- Improves communication between hospitals and authorities.
- Enables secure and efficient organ allocation.
